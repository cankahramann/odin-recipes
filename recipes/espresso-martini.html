Espresso Martini

Description

According to legend, this caffeinated cocktail was created in the 1980s specifically for supermodel Naomi Campbell. With its delicious coffee flavor and power to fuel revelry into the wee hours, the espresso martini was due for a comeback.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz. vodka
  • 1 oz. espresso
  • 1 oz. Kahlua
  • 1/2 tbsp. simple syrup
  • Cocoa powder and espresso beans, for serving
    •

    Steps

    1. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, shake all ingredients until cold and foamy.
    2. Strain into coupe glass. Cover half of top of glass with paper towel and dust other half with cocoa powder. Add espresso beans to plain side of glass if desired.
