Irish Cream

Description

With this sweet and creamy liqueur recipe, you're only a DIY away from homemade Baileys.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 c. heavy cream
  • 1 c. Irish whiskey (or milk for a nonalcoholic version)
  • 2 tbsp. chocolate syrup
  • 1 1/2 tsp. instant coffee or espresso granules
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp. almond extract
    •

    Steps

    1. Blend sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, Irish whiskey or milk, chocolate syrup, instant coffee or espresso granules, vanilla extract and almond extract until smooth.
    2. Add a splash to coffee, pour it over ice cream or sip it on the rocks.
