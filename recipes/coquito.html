Coquito

Description

Get in the holiday spirit with this creamy Puerto Rican rum drink made with a trio of milks including coconut milk (coquito is “little coconut” in Spanish). Sweet and rich with warming spices, it's reminiscent of eggnog—but without the egg. Bottled coquito will keep in the fridge for up to 1 month, so it makes a delightful edible gift for the cocktail aficionado in your life. Salud!

Ingredients

  • 1 15-ounce can coconut milk
  • 1 14-ounce can condensed milk
  • 1 12-ounce can evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 c. light rum
    •

    Steps

    1. In blender, puree coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg to combine.
    2. Stir in rum, transfer to bottles or pitcher, and refrigerate overnight or up to 1 month. Makes 5 1/2 cups.
